Doom Testament (summary):
The Slayer was once a mortal warrior who stood against the forces of Hell without hesitation. Through endless battles he became a living weapon of vengeance. The demons feared him, naming him the Doom Slayer. Sealed away by the dark lords after they could not kill him, he remained entombed until the age when Hell rose again. When awakened, he resumed the eternal war, driven not by glory but by relentless, unyielding retribution.
